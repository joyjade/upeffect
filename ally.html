We’re building a funding pot for social entrepreneurs

To advance this work, we’re building a funding pot for social entrepreneurs. 100% of donations made are allocated to UpEffect campaigns. This funding helps founders meet their goals.

As an UpEffect Ally, your donation will advance opportunities for social ventures. We screen all companies against our internal criteria which checks for team credibility, community investment, research, impact and financials. We have also made a commitment to work with businesses prioritising economic opportunities for underserved communities.

UpEffect Allies will receive annual impact reports on how their donations have translated into wonderful opportunities for the community.

How it Works

The Ally pot is distributed with input from our donors: you. Each Ally has one vote and voting sessions will be called to decide on the allocation of these funds e.g. amount being awarded and campaigns being rewarded. There will be a ledger available to all Ally donors for full visibility.

The model is inspired by Kwanda. Contribute to their pot for Black communities here.
