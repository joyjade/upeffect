a podcast by UpEffect

Highlighting the emerging need for responsible trade that uplifts communities frequently left behind. In each episode, we’ll invite innovators and thought leaders to deconstruct conventional systems perpetuating economic inequality and spotlight models that are re-envisioning business. Together we’ll unearth a blueprint for an economy redesign.

What People Are Saying

I’ve been very grateful for this path Sheeza and UpEffect are opening for discovery of the global solidarity movement and its ways ancient and new. Each episode is a wonderful window on ideas, ventures and lives in a sense familiar, in that I recognize a common transformational purpose and hope in the voices I’m hearing, and at the same time unfamiliar, revealing leaders and practitioners unknown to me and initiatives from communities much outside the dominant-culture story. A happy addition to regular listening!

Each episode has taught me so much and given me ideas I can implement in my daily life to counteract the injustices that are all too common. Highly recommend this podcast if you want to learn the true impact of the broken systems we live in and have practical tips on how we can change them.
